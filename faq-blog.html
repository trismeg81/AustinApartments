Neighborhood Spotlights

South Lamar (SoLa)

Trendy, walkable, and packed with food trucks, bars, and new apartments. Great for creatives and young professionals. I love this area for access to nature as well, with Zilker Park, Barton Springs, the Greenbelt, Lady Bird Lake, you've got great access to all you could ask for.

East Austin

Culturally rich and rapidly growing. You’ll find everything from art murals to breweries and cozy studios to luxury lofts. I used to live in New York City, and closer to downtown on the Eastside is comparable to Williamsburg in Brooklyn. Furthr out East is a great up and coming area that gives you a little more peace, but there's great new restaurants, bars, breweries, coffee shops, and activities out here now.

North Burnet / Domain

Modern, tech-heavy zone perfect for professionals. Live close to Apple, Amazon, and The Domain with luxury living options. Our Major League Soccer team plays out here at Q2 stadium.
